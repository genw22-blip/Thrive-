Cortisol tips
Morning light
Stand near a window within 30 min of waking. Anchors your cortisol curve and dramatically improves sleep onset.
Delay coffee 90 min
Cortisol peaks naturally 30–60 min after waking. Coffee on top spikes it further. Wait until 7am+ to blunt the cortisol load.
Post-meal walk
Even 10 min after Meal 1 or 3 lowers blood glucose, blunts cortisol, and is the easiest movement entry point when energy is low.
Hard stop 8pm
Screens off or blue-light glasses. Cortisol can't drop if your nervous system stays stimulated. Poor sleep = next-day inflammation.
Lemon + salt water
16oz filtered water + juice of ½ lemon + pinch Celtic sea salt on waking. Supports adrenals and blunts morning cortisol spike.
